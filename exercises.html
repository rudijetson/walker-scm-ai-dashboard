Hands-On Exercises

Practice using AI with real Walker SCM scenarios

Each exercise walks you through a real-world scenario using prompts and sample data from the Training Kit. Beginner exercises use a single prompt. Intermediate may require iteration. Advanced exercises combine multiple prompts into a complete workflow.
1

Analyze an RFP

Beginner 15-20 min
Scenario: You've received an RFP from Sunrise Furniture Co. looking for a 3PL partner. Your sales manager wants a quick summary of what they're asking for and whether we should pursue it.
1.
Open RFP Analysis Prompts

Find "Prompt 1: Basic RFP Extraction"

Open RFP Analysis Prompts →
2.
Get the Sample RFP

Copy the sample RFP content

Open Sample RFP →
3.
Run in ChatGPT or Claude

Paste the prompt, then paste the RFP where indicated

4.
Review Output

Check for customer info, services, volumes, timeline, and follow-up questions

Success Criteria

  • Customer name and contact identified
  • Services requested clearly listed
  • Volume estimates extracted
  • Key dates noted

3

Write a Delay Notification

Intermediate 15 min
Scenario: A shipment is delayed due to carrier breakdown. The customer (3-year account, $45K/month) previously complained about not being notified promptly. Write a professional email that's empathetic but not excessive.
1.
Open Customer Communication Prompts

Find "Scenario A: Shipment Delay Notification"

Open Customer Communication Prompts →
2.
Run and evaluate

Does it acknowledge their previous feedback? Is the tone right?

3.
Iterate

Ask the AI to make it shorter, more apologetic, or add a discount offer

Success Criteria

  • Email under 200 words
  • Acknowledges previous feedback
  • Provides clear ETA
  • Professional tone
Tip: Try the same prompt in both ChatGPT and Claude to compare outputs.
