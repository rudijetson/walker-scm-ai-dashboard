Writing & Communication

Professional Email Response

Use when: You need to respond to a client or internal email professionally

I received this email: [Paste the email here] Help me write a professional response that: - Acknowledges their concern - Provides a clear answer or next steps - Maintains a helpful, professional tone - Is concise (under 150 words)
Tip: Always review AI-generated emails before sending. Add personal touches and verify any facts or commitments.

Meeting Summary

Use when: You need to summarize meeting notes into action items

Here are my meeting notes: [Paste notes here] Create a structured meeting summary with: 1. Key decisions made 2. Action items (with owners if mentioned) 3. Open questions or follow-ups needed 4. Next meeting date/topic if discussed Format it so I can paste it into an email to attendees.

Prompting Tips

The CRAFT Framework

A simple structure for effective prompts

Context - What's the situation? Who's the audience?

Role - What role should the AI play? (analyst, writer, advisor)

Action - What specific task do you need done?

Format - How should the output be structured?

Tone - What's the appropriate voice? (formal, casual, technical)

Iterative Prompting

Don't expect perfection on the first try

Start broad, then narrow:

  • First prompt: Get the general direction
  • Second prompt: "Make it more [specific quality]"
  • Third prompt: "Now adjust [specific section]"

Useful follow-ups:

  • "Make this shorter/longer"
  • "Make this more formal/casual"
  • "Add more detail to section X"
  • "Give me 3 alternative versions"
  • "What questions should I be asking?"
