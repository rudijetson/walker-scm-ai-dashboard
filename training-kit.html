Prompt Structure (Four Building Blocks)

ROLE

Who is the AI acting as?

"You are a senior logistics account manager..."

CONTEXT

What background does it need?

"This customer has been with us for 3 years..."

TASK

What should it do?

"Write a professional email explaining the delay..."

FORMAT

How should output look?

"Use bullet points. Keep under 200 words."
